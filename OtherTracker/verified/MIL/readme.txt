How to reproduce the results in our paper?

Just run the run_xxxTRACKER.m to start the tracker. Notice that the image files will be in the ./LaSOTBenchmark/ (see the benchmark_path in the script)

The structure of the ./LaSOTBenchmark/ is as follows:
./LaSOTBenchmark/
        --| airplane/
          --| img/
              --| 00000001.jpg
          --| groundtruth.txt
		  
If you use the code and tracking results on LaSOT for your research, please kindly cite the original tracking paper, and our benchmark paper:

[1] H. Fan*, L. Lin*, F. Yang*, P. Chu*, G. Deng, S. Yu, H. Bai, Y. Xu, C. Liao, and H. Ling. LaSOT: A High-quality Benchmark for Large-scale Single Object Tracking, arXiv, 2018.
